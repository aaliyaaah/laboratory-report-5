# Experiment 1: Full Demodulation of a QPSK Signal

## Objective
To implement and analyze the full demodulation of a QPSK signal, including verification of data conversion, signal generation, channel modeling, and the effects of noise on recovered digital data.

## Equipment Limitation
The Phase Adjust module of the Emona Telecoms Trainer used in this experiment was not functioning properly. As a result, accurate phase synchronization could not be achieved. This limitation affected signal recovery, particularly in Parts D and E, where deviations from expected results were observed.

## Parts of the Experiment
- Part A: Verifying the operation of parallel-to-serial then serial-to-parallel conversion 
- Part B: Generating a QPSK signal 
- Part C: Modelling the channel conditions
- Part D: Full demodulation of a QPSK signal 
- Part E: Observation of channel noise on the recovered digital data
